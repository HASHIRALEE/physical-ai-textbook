## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to the field of artificial intelligence that focuses on embodied intelligence - AI systems that interact with the physical world through robotic bodies. This encompasses everything from simple mobile robots to complex humanoid systems that can perceive, reason, and act in real-world environments.

## Key Concepts

- Embodied cognition: The idea that intelligence emerges from the interaction between an agent and its environment
- Sensorimotor integration: Combining sensory input with motor actions to achieve goals
- Real-world reasoning: Making decisions based on physical constraints and affordances
